HOW TO MAKE TEA
  1. Boil Water in an utensil.
  2. Add Tea leaves in it.
  3. Add Sugar in it.
  4. After it has boiled, add milk.
  5. Add Cardamom/Ginger for enhaced taste.
  6. Boil until color changes.
  7. Serve it hot with rusk or bread.
